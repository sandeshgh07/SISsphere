# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, and disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that harms the community.
